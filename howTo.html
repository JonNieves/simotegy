Map and Objective

Map

There are 3 areas of note on the map:

  1. Blue Team Base
  2. Red Team Base
  3. Goal Area (center)

Players in their own bases are safe from collisions with spikes

Blue starts here
Get in and stay in here
Red starts here
Objective

The objective for both teams is to have more players occupying the central area than the opposing team for a time. The challenge is to do it while avoiding increasing numbers of spikes.

Player Roles

There are 2 player roles:

1) Leaders

2) Members

Leaders are larger than Members and act as mobile respawn points for some Members.

Player Controls

Keyboard and Mouse

Press W to go Up, A to go Left, S to go Down, D to go Right

Double-press any of them for a Speed Boost

Pressing or or or launches a spike in the pointed direction

Clicking the mouse (relative to the player model) also launches a spike in that direction

Touchscreen (partially supported :()

Swipe anywhere in the direction you want to go

Tapping the screen (relative to the player model) launches a spike in that direction

Player Abilities

Speed Boosting

Players can double-press W/A/S/D to double their velocity

The increased velocity affects spikes generated

Generating Spikes

Spikes are hazardous entities that knock out players they come into contact with.

Fullscreen Inverted Colors Bug on Chrome

The fix for this issue is to disable the hardware acceleration setting in Chrome.

Menu -> Settings -> Advanced -> [disable] Use Hardware Acceleration When Available
